Rushing Water

"You want me to what?

"I don't want you to do anything. He wants you to come with us. You want to come with us. So I'm going to need you to jump."

He began to tap his fingers along the bridge, legs swinging back and forth as he stared at me.

"You don't like me. I get it, but you could be a little more helpful," I marched towards him and placed my hands on my hips. "I didn't ask you to come help me, so if you aren't going to be helpful you can leave."

He laughed. The biscuit laughed at me, and if it hadn't been for the fact my mama told me not to hit people who are laughing I'd have punched him.

"You want help darling?," he stood up and walked towards me. "Here." Next thing I knew I was falling over the bridge.
